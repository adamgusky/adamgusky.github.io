About Us

Good friends, great coffee—it’s not just our motto; it’s who we are.

We’re about building relationships with our customers and the local community. Step through the door of Cool Beans and you’ll be greeted with a genuine smile by our baristas who are ready to customize your drink so it’s just the way you like it. Cool Beans is a local business that supports local businesses. We’re committed to finding the best products and ingredients for our customers from local sources. When you grab a coffee from us you’re not just supporting one local business but the many other we partner with. And then there’s the coffee…sourced entirely from local roasters, sure to please even the most discerning coffee connoisseur.
